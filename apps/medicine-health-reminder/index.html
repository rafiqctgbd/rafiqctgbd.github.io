What It Does

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for real-world medicine management — precise alarms, private records, and optional cloud backup.

⏰

Precise Medicine Alarms

Exact-time reminders that fire reliably even after device restarts, with a gently rising alarm volume so wake-up is comfortable, not jarring. Daily, weekly, weekdays, weekends, monthly — all supported.

📊

Dose History & Missed Detection

Full history of taken, snoozed, and missed doses. Unanswered alarms auto-snooze automatically and are marked as a missed dose after 3 unanswered reminders — no more endless ringing.

⚖️

Health Tracker — Weight, BP & Sugar

Log your weight with auto-calculated BMI, and track blood pressure and blood sugar readings over time. A simple personal trend log, kept entirely on your device.

📋

Medical Records

Store health notes, medical records, and image attachments. Share directly via WhatsApp or email when needed.

☁️

Google Drive Backup

Optional backup to your own private Google Drive AppData folder. Restore reminders and records across devices anytime.

⏸️

Pause All Reminders

Travelling or admitted to hospital? Pause all reminders temporarily with one tap and resume when ready.

🔐

Secure Local Storage

All data stored locally on your device by default. No developer servers. No ads. No data collection.

🏥

Health Checkup Reminders

Annual medical checkups, dental visits, eye exams, vaccinations — schedule any health reminder yearly, monthly, or as a one-time alert.

💉

Vaccination & Appointment Tracking

Never miss a booster shot, follow-up appointment, or specialist referral. Set once and let the app remind you at the right time.

🔍

Medicine Info, One Tap Away

Tap any reminder to search that medicine online, or look up its generic name, dosage form, and manufacturer from a built-in Bangladesh medicine database — quick reference, never medical advice from within the app.

📤

Share Your Schedule

Send your full medicine schedule as a text message via WhatsApp or SMS — handy for keeping a family member or caregiver in the loop.

Privacy First

Your health data
stays yours

This app was built with a simple principle: your medical information is private. No advertising SDKs, no data collection, no developer servers storing your records.
