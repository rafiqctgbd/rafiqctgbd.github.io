What It Does

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for real-world medicine management — precise alarms, private records, and optional cloud backup.

⏰

Precise Medicine Alarms

Exact-time reminders that fire reliably even after device restarts. Snooze, take, or mark missed — all tracked.

📋

Health Records

Store health notes, medical records, and image attachments. Share via WhatsApp or email when needed.

📊

Dose History Tracking

Full history of taken, snoozed, and missed doses. Understand your adherence patterns over time.

☁️

Google Drive Backup

Optional backup to your own private Google Drive AppData folder. Restore across devices anytime.

⏸️

Pause All Reminders

Travelling or in a meeting? Pause all reminders temporarily with one tap and resume when ready.

🔐

Secure Local Storage

All data stored locally on your device by default. No developer servers. No ads. No data selling.

Privacy First

Your health data
stays yours

This app was built with a simple principle: your medical information is private. No advertising SDKs, no data collection, no servers storing your records.

  • Data stored locally on device by default

  • Zero advertisements, zero ad SDKs
