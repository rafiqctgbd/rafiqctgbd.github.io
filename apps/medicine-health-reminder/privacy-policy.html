01 Overview

Medicine & Health Reminder ("the App") helps users manage medicine schedules, health reminders, and personal medical records. User privacy is the core principle of this application — your health data stays on your device.

02 Information We Collect

The App may store the following information:

  • Medicine schedules and reminder settings
  • Health-related notes and records
  • Medical images or attachments added by the user
  • App preferences and settings
  • Dose history log (taken, snoozed, or missed — stored locally on device only)
  • Health Tracker entries — weight/BMI, blood pressure, and blood sugar readings (stored locally on device only)

By default, all information is stored locally on the user's own device only. The App does not upload or store user data on developer-owned servers.

If the user chooses to sign in with Google and use the optional backup feature, backup data is stored only inside the user's own private Google Drive AppData folder. This is completely optional and requires explicit user consent.

03 Google Sign-In & Drive Access

The App optionally allows users to sign in with Google to backup and restore their medicine reminders and health records.

The App uses the Google Drive drive.appdata scope only. This means:

  • Data is stored privately in the user's own Google Drive account
  • Data is not shared with third parties
  • Data is not sold
  • Only the user can access their backup data
  • The App cannot access general Google Drive files, photos, videos, or documents

04 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with any third parties under any circumstances.

The App includes two user-initiated actions that involve external apps or services:

  • Search this medicine online — opens your device's default browser with a general web search for the medicine name. This is governed by your browser's and Google's own privacy policy; the App does not collect or store this search query.
  • Share schedule / Share records — uses Android's standard share menu so you can send your medicine schedule or records (e.g. via WhatsApp, SMS, or email) to a recipient of your choice. The App does not transmit this data to any server — you choose the destination app each time.

05 User Control

  • Users may use the app without Google Sign-In
  • Users may revoke Google account access anytime through Google Account settings
  • Users may delete all backups from Google Drive directly within the App
  • Users may uninstall the app to remove all local device data

06 Permissions Used

  • Internet access — backup and restore via Google Drive only
  • Notifications and alarms — medicine reminder alerts
  • Camera and media/storage access — medical image attachments
  • Boot completed — automatically restore reminders after device restart
  • Exact alarm scheduling — ensure reminders fire at precise times

07 Data Retention

Backup data remains in the user's own Google Drive account until the user chooses to delete it. The App developer does not retain copies of user backup data.

08 Advertising

This application does not display advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs. No user data is used for advertising purposes.

09 Children's Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the App or applicable regulations. Users will be informed of significant changes via app update release notes. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
