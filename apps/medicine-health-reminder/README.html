📋 About

Medicine & Health Reminder is designed for people who need reliable, private medicine schedule management on Android. All data lives on your device by default — no accounts required, no data collection, no ads.

✨ Features

  • Medicine reminder alarms with exact-time scheduling
  • Health tracking notes and personal records
  • Medical record management with image attachments
  • Dose history tracking — taken, snoozed, and missed
  • Pause all reminders temporarily with one tap
  • Share medical records via WhatsApp or email
  • Google Drive backup and restore (optional)
  • Secure local data storage — no server required
  • Notification reminders with reliable delivery
  • Optional Google Sign-In support

🔐 Data Privacy

User privacy is the core principle of this application.

  • Data stored locally on the user's own device by default
  • No personal data stored on developer-owned servers
  • Google Drive backup is completely optional
  • Backup stored only in the user's own private Google Drive AppData folder
  • User data is never sold or shared with third parties
  • No advertisements — no advertising SDKs used

☁️ Google Drive Backup

The application optionally supports Google Drive backup and restore using the Google Drive scope:

drive.appdata

This allows users to securely backup and restore medicine reminders and health records across devices while keeping data private and isolated from normal Google Drive files.

The app cannot access general Google Drive files, photos, videos, or documents — only the user's own AppData folder.

🛡️ Permissions Used

🌐 Internet Access
Backup and restore via Google Drive only
🔔 Notifications & Alarms
Medicine reminder alerts at scheduled times
📷 Camera & Storage
Medical image attachments in health records
🔁 Boot Completed
Restore reminders automatically after restart
⏱️ Exact Alarm Scheduling
Ensures reminders fire at the precise scheduled time, not approximately
